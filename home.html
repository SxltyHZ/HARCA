The Area Roofing Contractors Association (HARCA) is a regional chapter of the Roofing Contractors Association of Texas (RCAT), representing the voice of the roofing and waterproofing industry in Houston and nearby areas.

We support contractors with continuing education, networking, and industry advocacy, while also promoting consumer awareness and high standards across the field.
